About Synergy School of Engineering

Synergy School of Engineering is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation among students. Our mission is to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen fields.

We offer a variety of programs and resources to support student success and encourage lifelong learning.

1 . College Overview

Synergy School of Engineering is a technical institution situated at Banamali Prasaad, Dhenkanal. It aims to provide quality engineering education to students from various regions. The college focuses on academic excellence, professional development, and practical learning in engineering fields. 2. Available Departments

The college offers five major engineering branches to cater to different technical domains. These departments include Computer Science Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mining Engineering. Each department is equipped with subject-specific faculty and resources to support in-depth learning and innovation

3. Hostel Facilities

Synergy School of Engineering provides separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls. The hostels are designed to offer a safe, comfortable, and disciplined living environment for students coming from distant locations. The hostel facilities aim to make students feel at home while also encouraging academic focus and peer collaboration.

4. Canteen and Food Services

A well-maintained canteen operates within the college premises, offering hygienic and affordable food options for students and staff. It serves as a social space for students to relax and refresh during breaks. The availability of quality meals ensures that students remain energized throughout the day

5. Library and Study Resources

The college features a central library with a wide collection of academic books, journals, and digital resources. Students from all departments can access materials relevant to their curriculum. The library supports independent learning and research work, which is an essential part of technical education

6. Playground and Sports Activities

To promote physical fitness and overall development, the college provides a spacious playground. It supports various sports and extracurricular activities, encouraging students to maintain a balance between academics and recreation. Sports events and competitions are regularly organized to engage students beyond classrooms

7. Departmental Management Project Overview

The Departmental Management Project is a web-based system developed to manage and organize information across all departments and facilities of the college. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the system includes modules for managing department-wise data, student and faculty records, hostel details, and facility information. The project enhances operational efficiency, reduces paperwork, and supports digital transformation in academic management
