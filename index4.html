Meet Our Faculty

At Synergy School of Engineering, our faculty members are dedicated professionals with extensive experience in their respective fields. They are committed to providing quality education and mentoring to our students.

Our faculty members are not only educators but also researchers and industry experts who bring real-world experience into the classroom.

Principal of Synergy school of Engineering


Pabitra Kumar Nayak is the esteemed Principal of Synergy School of Engineering,
providing visionary leadership and academic excellence to the institution. With a deep commitment to education and innovation, he plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of aspiring engineers. Under his guidance, the college has flourished as a hub of technical knowledge and skill development, fostering an environment where students are encouraged to excel in academics, research, and extracurricular activities. With a strong academic background and years of experience in the field of education, Pabitra Kumar Nayak ensures that Synergy School of Engineering maintains high standards in teaching, infrastructure, and industry collaboration. His leadership emphasizes discipline, teamwork, and holistic development, preparing students to face real-world challenges with confidence. He actively promotes modern teaching methodologies, cutting-edge technologies, and hands-on learning approaches, ensuring that students gain practical exposure along with theoretical knowledge. Beyond academics, he is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and research, encouraging students and faculty to engage in projects that contribute to technological advancements. His relentless efforts and progressive vision continue to inspire both students and educators, making Synergy School of Engineering a premier institution for quality engineering education
